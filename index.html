WALL FEATURES

Geometrical

Contemporary designs and vintage-pop references. Large circles, rings that intersect in an interplay of psychedelic references.

Jewels

Designs inspired by faceted cuts that generate the sparkling of the diamond. By the soft, round cut of the cabochon. By the preciousness of stones, by the light of gold.

Natural

Designs inspired by flowers and animals. Macro in anthropomorphic forms, patterns of leaves seen under the microscope and illuminated by the sun, immense, delicate corollas. Reeds in the wind.

Oriental

Designs that approach the archetypes of the East. From the sinuous leaf of Cashmere design, created in Mesopotamia, to ancient Japanese fans, to the perfect geometries of the Arab world.
