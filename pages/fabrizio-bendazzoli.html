    Fabrizio Bendazzoli was born in Verona in 1988 and currently resides permanently in Milan. In 2014, he graduated with honors in Product Design from the Nuova Accademia di Belle Arti in Milan (NABA).

    Since 2015, he has been collaborating with a well-known Milanese architecture and design studio, providing his experience and ideas on various projects. In 2019, he decided to open his multidisciplinary studio, where he can finally dedicate himself to creating exclusive projects that reflect his vision and creative soul.

    Fabrizio's design is characterized by its graphics, cleanliness, and succinctness, with particular attention to the world of luxury. Each of his projects is conceived as a focal point for interpersonal situations and relationships, as a relationship with the built and natural world. In this way, all the peculiarities of his character are reflected in his work: care, attention to detail, rigor, and passion for luxury.

    His contemporary aesthetic is highly recognizable and visually impactful, thanks to the great refinement of the product, the strength of its identity, and the incredible functionality of each element that is part of it. His design methodology and personal approach to work give him exceptional effectiveness in perceiving, developing, and guiding the project in all its aspects, paying particular attention to the needs of the most demanding client.

    Platform magazine elected Fabrizio Bendazzoli as one of the "Best Italian architects and designers under 40" in 2020, confirming his talent in the world of Italian design.
